Tech Cafe Data Analysis

This project analyzes the relationship between money spent and waiting time at a tech cafe. The data was collected through a survey and analyzed using Python. Below are the insights and visualizations.

Data Summary

  • Mean Money Spent: $60.24
  • Mean Waiting Time: 15.73 minutes
  • Standard Deviation (Money Spent): $41.60
  • Standard Deviation (Waiting Time): 10.89 minutes
  • Correlation between Money Spent and Waiting Time: 0.98 (Strong Positive Correlation)

Distribution of Money Spent and Waiting Time

  • Money Spent: The distribution is right-skewed, with most customers spending between $20 and $100.
  • Waiting Time: The distribution is also right-skewed, with most customers waiting between 5 and 25 minutes.

Correlation Analysis

  • Scatter Plot: Shows a strong positive linear relationship between money spent and waiting time.
  • Correlation Heatmap: Confirms the strong correlation (0.98) between the two variables.

Linear Regression Model

  • Intercept: 1.23
  • Slope: 0.24
  • Insight: For every dollar spent, waiting time increases by 0.24 minutes.

Conclusion

The analysis reveals a strong positive correlation between money spent and waiting time at the tech cafe. Customers who spend more tend to wait longer, likely due to more complex orders. The linear regression model further quantifies this relationship.
